# Refund Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

---

## 1. General Policy

Due to the nature of custom software development and digital product delivery, all payments are non-refundable once work has commenced. Before initiating any project, we ensure clear agreement on scope, deliverables, and timeline to minimize the risk of misalignment.

---

## 2. Project Payments

### 2.1 Advance Payment

A 50% advance payment is required before work begins on any project. This payment covers initial scoping, resource allocation, and setup costs.

### 2.2 Refund Eligibility

- **Before work begins:** Full refund of advance payment if you cancel before we start work
- **During project:** No refund of advance payment. Work completed is billed at the agreed rate
- **On delivery:** Remaining 50% is non-refundable once paid and deliverables provided

### 2.3 Cancellation During Project

If you cancel after work has begun:
- All completed milestones up to the cancellation date will be billed
- Any unused portion of the advance payment will be applied against final invoice
- No further payments will be due beyond completed work

---

## 3. Monthly Retainers

Monthly retainer payments are billed in advance on the 1st of each month. Retainer fees are:

- **Non-refundable** once the billing month begins
- **Prorated** for partial months at the start or end of an engagement

---

## 4. Subscription Products

### 4.1 ORACLE / SHADOW / Other Subscription Products

- Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time before the next billing cycle
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
- No prorated refunds for partial months
- Annual subscriptions: refund calculated on unused months, minus a 15% administrative fee

### 4.2 Free Trials

If a free trial is offered, it converts to a paid subscription at the end of the trial period unless cancelled before. Ensure you cancel before the trial ends if you do not wish to continue.

---

## 5. Dispute Resolution

If you believe a billing error has occurred, contact us within 7 days of the charge at signal@metafloor.in. We will investigate and resolve legitimate errors promptly.

---

## 6. Exceptions

We handle exceptional circumstances — such as complete project failure due to our fault — on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss.

---

Billing questions and refund requests: signal@metafloor.in
