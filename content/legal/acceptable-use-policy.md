## 2. Prohibited Uses

You may not use our services to:

### 2.1 Illegal Activities

- Engage in any activity that violates applicable local, national, or international laws
- Facilitate illegal acts or the planning of illegal acts
- Traffic in illegal goods or services

### 2.2 Harm to Others

- Harm, harass, intimidate, or threaten any individual or group
- Incite violence or hatred
- Engage in cyberstalking or harassment of any kind

### 2.3 Fraud and Misrepresentation

- Impersonate any person or entity
- Misrepresent your affiliation with any person or entity
- Make false statements to obtain services or information

### 2.4 Security Violations

- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems or any third-party systems
- Introduce malware, viruses, or any malicious code
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of our infrastructure without authorization
- Circumvent any security or authentication measures

### 2.5 Data Harvesting

- Collect information about others without their consent
- Use automated tools to scrape, harvest, or extract data from our platform
- Scrape email addresses or phone numbers for unsolicited communications

### 2.6 Spam and Unsolicited Communication

- Send unsolicited commercial messages (spam)
- Use our services to distribute malware or unwanted software
- Use our services to conduct phishing or fraud

### 2.7 Intellectual Property Violations

- Infringe on the copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights of others
- Distribute pirated software, media, or other proprietary material
- Provide access to tools designed to circumvent copyright protection mechanisms

### 2.8 Product-Specific Restrictions

**ORACLE Signals:**
- Do not use signals for insider trading or market manipulation
- Do not republish or redistribute signals without explicit agreement

**SHADOW Analysis:**
- Do not use analysis for harassment or blackmail
- Do not publish findings without appropriate context and disclaimers

**OUTBOUND Voice AI:**
- Do not use for unsolicited marketing calls (spam)
- Do not use for robocalls in violation of applicable telemarketing regulations (TRAI DND regulations in India, FTC TSR in the US, or equivalent local laws)
- Ensure compliance with all applicable telemarketing and calling regulations
