Drafting notes:
1. The prompt contains an internal inconsistency on operator-sourced service projects. The narrative rule says a 10% referral commission is paid from Metafloor's side, but the worked example yields a 39% operator / 61% Metafloor split. The Contractor Services Agreement is drafted to the narrative rule by default and includes alternate fallback language for the 39/61 outcome.
2. The Product Revenue Share Agreement preserves lifetime 49/51 economics for the specific voice agent product only; it does not grant rights in other products.
3. The SOW template is drafted to work under either the Contractor Services Agreement or the Product Revenue Share Agreement.
4. These drafts should be reviewed by India-qualified counsel before signature, especially for stamp duty, GST / TDS, and any FEMA-linked payment handling.
